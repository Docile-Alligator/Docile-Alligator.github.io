Infinity for Reddit Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

Infinity for Reddit has zero tolerance for content or interactions that involve the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. While our app does not host or store content directly, it enables access to Reddit’s platform through their official API. All content displayed in Infinity for Reddit originates from Reddit and is subject to Reddit’s policies and enforcement measures.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Infinity for Reddit follows Reddit’s strict policies prohibiting:

  • Any sexual or suggestive content involving minors (under 18 years
    old)
  • Any attempts to solicit, distribute, or engage in predatory behavior
    towards minors
  • Content depicting or promoting neglect, physical, or emotional abuse
    of minors

We stand firmly against any form of child exploitation and are committed to ensuring a safe browsing experience.

How Child Sexual Exploitation Content Is Handled

Since all content in Infinity for Reddit is provided through Reddit’s API, we rely on Reddit’s enforcement mechanisms to detect and remove such violations. Reddit utilizes:

  • Industry-leading hash-matching technology (e.g., PhotoDNA, YouTube
    CSAI Match) to detect and remove known child sexual abuse material
    (CSAM).
  • Automated content detection methods to identify high-risk
    interactions that could lead to child exploitation.
  • Reports from child safety organizations like the National Center for
    Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • User reporting mechanisms to flag inappropriate content for review
    and removal.

When Reddit detects CSAM, they immediately remove the content, ban the violating accounts, and report it to law enforcement as required by law.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content on Infinity for Reddit that you believe involves child sexual exploitation, please report it directly to Reddit by using any of the option:

  • Using Infinity for Reddit’s in-app reporting option to flag the
    content and user.
  • Submitting a report via Reddit’s website or app. To know how, read here.

If you are a victim or need support, you can reach out to:

In the US:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC):
    CyberTipline
  • National Sexual Assault Hotline (U.S.): 1-800-656-HOPE
  • Childhelp (U.S.): 1-800-4ACHILD

Outside the US:

  • Child Helpline International

Continuous Efforts

Infinity for Reddit remains dedicated to providing a safe browsing experience by adhering to Reddit’s strict enforcement measures. We support efforts to combat child sexual abuse and exploitation and encourage our users to report any suspicious or harmful activity.

For more information, please refer to Reddit’s policies on child sexual exploitation.
